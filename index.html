Things to do in Bohol

Bohol is a tropical paradise with a lot of activities to offer. Here are some of the things you can do while you are in Bohol: How far away and how to get to each from Oceanica.

Visit the Chocolate Hills. The most famous attraction in Bohol. The Chocolate Hills are a geological formation of over 1200 hills over an area of more than 50 square kilometers. The hills are covered in green grass that turns brown during the dry season, hence the name.
Swim with the whale sharks
The Tarsier Sanctuary. The Philippine tarsier is one of the smallest primates in the world. These tiny creatures have large eyes and are very sensitive to light. They are nocturnal and primarily found in the forests of Bohol.
The Loboc River cruise. Famous for its serene cruises. Guests can enjoy a buffet meal on a floating restaurant while being serenaded by local musicians. The cruise passes through lush greenery and picturesque scenery.
